About the Database

This database collects and collates information on microbial stress resistance elements, aiming to provide researchers with genetic sequence information on microbial adaptation or resistance to adverse environmental conditions under various stresses. They contain different types of stress resistance elements, such as transcription factors, regulatory elements, response genes, or functional proteins.

Features:

  • Searchable gene information
  • Gene sequence display
  • Pagination for large datasets
